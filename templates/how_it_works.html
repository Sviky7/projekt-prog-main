How It Works

1. Sign Up or Log In

To get started, either sign up for a new account or log in to your existing account. If you are a tutor, be sure to create a tutor profile after signing up.

2. Search for Tutors or Subjects

Use the search feature to find tutors or browse through popular subjects. You can filter tutors by subject to find the best match for your needs.

3. Book a Session or Create a Profile

As a student, you can book a session with your chosen tutor. If you're a tutor, create a detailed profile to offer your services and start booking sessions.

4. Attend the Session

Once your session is booked, attend the session and learn from experienced tutors or teach your students if you're a tutor.

5. Leave Feedback

After each session, leave feedback for your tutor or student to help improve the experience for future users.
