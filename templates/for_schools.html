For Schools

ZochovaEdu offers a convenient platform for schools to manage tutors, create profiles, and ensure quality educational experiences. Here's how your school can get involved:

1. Sign Up Your School

Register your school on ZochovaEdu. Provide basic information about your school and the subjects you wish to offer through our platform.

2. Add Tutors

Once your school is registered, you can add your tutors to the platform. Each tutor can create their own profile to share their expertise and availability.

3. Customize Subjects

Schools can offer a wide range of subjects. Customize your offerings and add specialized subjects based on your school's curriculum to attract more students.

4. Manage Bookings

Manage and track tutor bookings through the platform. Allow students to book sessions with your tutors based on their availability and subject expertise.

5. Monitor Progress & Feedback

Track the progress of students and tutors. Gather feedback from both tutors and students to improve the quality of education your school offers on ZochovaEdu.
